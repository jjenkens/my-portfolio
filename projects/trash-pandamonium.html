Trash Pandamonium

Programmer · Capstone project
A top-down party game about cleaning up the environment, built as a University of Utah capstone project and released on Steam.

Overview

Trash Pandamonium is a top-down party game where players clean up trash, earn stat boosts, and compete in a final minigame to determine the winner.

My Role

I worked mainly as a programmer, with a focus on AI players so smaller groups could still experience the full four-player game.
