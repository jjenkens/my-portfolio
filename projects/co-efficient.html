Co-Efficient

Game developer · 8-week class project
A cooperative platformer where two players share one keyboard and mouse to solve character-driven puzzles.

Overview

Co-Efficient is a co-op platformer created with a small student team over 8 weeks. The game uses one shared keyboard and mouse, so each player has to coordinate closely.

Gameplay

Players control Coco, an elf who can shoot arrows and jump higher, and Effie, a wizard who can invert gravity and fit into tighter spaces. The puzzle design asks both characters to use their abilities together.

Scoring

After each puzzle, players earn stars based on arrow usage, gravity inversions, and whether they collected the level star, encouraging efficient solutions.
